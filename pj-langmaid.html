PJ Langmaid

PJ Langmaid, a former Fire Chief with 23 years of fire service, founded Chief Development Group to provide leadership coaching and consulting. With experience spanning front-line firefighting to executive roles, he rebuilt a community fire agency after Colorado’s most destructive wildfire. PJ’s no-nonsense approach helps teams align with their mission, vision, and values, enabling them to perform at exceptionally high standards. His focus is on cultivating leaders who dominate challenges.
