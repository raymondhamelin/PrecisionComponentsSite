Denny Giamazzo, a U.S. Army veteran and former Infantry Team Leader, channels his leadership experience into inspiring others through his podcast, “Be The Standard,” and public speaking. A Political Science graduate from UMASS Dartmouth, he transitioned to a successful career in software sales with companies like Oracle and Workday. Passionate about unlocking potential, Denny brings his insights on resilience and leadership to his work as an engagement speaker.

Hearing Denny Giamazzo’s story had a profound impact on me, despite our lives taking completely different paths. While Denny’s story is different from mine, it showed me that life can continue, and it’s how we choose to live each day that truly matters.
