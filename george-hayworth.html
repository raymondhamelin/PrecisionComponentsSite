George Hayworth

Leadership and Team BuildingMental Health and Emotional IntelligencePersonal Development
Book this asset

George is the founder and CEO of Elite Sentinel Coaching where he and his team Forge Leaders and Build Legacies. George is first and foremost a proud husband and father. He is a combat veteran who served on active duty as Field Artillery Officer from 2011-2016 with a deployment to Afghanistan. He continues to serve as an Engineer Officer in the Army National Guard. George also worked in the IT Services industry for nearly 10 years with global companies before starting his Leadership training company. His mission is to train and equip men to lead their families with strength and love.

Programs & Focus

Great leadership is more than just a service – it is a gift. George leverages his extensive military and civilian experience leading organizations ranging in size from 25-300 people to build leaders up as individuals for the benefit of their teams.

Balancing authority with responsibility and compassion is essential. It is not enough to merely know the tools, a leader must wield them with competence and grace. Plan effectively, cast vision clearly, and inspire others to maximize human potential.

Our mind can either be our greatest weapon or worst enemy. George helps leaders master the war within – to quiet the noise and replace fear and anxiety with confidence and peace.

Shedding limiting beliefs, mastering self-talk, creating alter egos, and leveraging a personal creed are powerful tools George trains to sharpen the mind and heart.

Emotional intelligence and empathy are the catalysts to great leadership and an unbreakable culture. Leaders connect constantly, listen patiently and decide effectively.

An organization’s strongest asset is its people. Survive together or fail as one. Great leaders empower their teams to work with each other towards the final objective. George trains leaders to build a deep and thriving culture built on vision and values. Delegation is essential and developing talent is a requirement for growth.
