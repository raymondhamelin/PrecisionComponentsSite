Nick Lavery

Nick is the founder and CEO of Precision Components LLC, an active-duty Green Beret in the U.S. Army Special Forces, and the best-selling author of Objective Secure. He is a proud husband, father, and is considered to be the first Special Forces operator to return to combat as an above-the-knee amputee. With a passion for leadership, resilience, and goal achievement, he has quickly become one of the most sought after speakers, mentors, and instructors in the country. Nick finds joy in fitness, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, business, and creating lasting memories with his wife and sons.

Areas of Focus

Unconventional Leadership places an emphasis on working "with and through" the people within an organization to create operational effects. This curriculum is designed to create leaders with a "Force Multiplier" mentality by focusing on essential leadership attributes such as: competence, relationships, mental toughness, communication, humility, disciplined initiative, mutual trust, and values.

Drawing from the Warrior Ethos, Nick illuminates the path to cultivating a resolute mindset. While rooted in military wisdom, this philosophy transcends boundaries, offering a transformative blueprint for individuals within organizations across all domains. The result? Unleashed potential and optimized productivity.

Nick's Objective Secure curriculum distills the essence of U.S. Army Special Forces' mission principles and operational approach into actionable strategies and tactics applicable in any industry. By embracing the phases and methodology, participants emerge not just as winners, but as architects of impact and transformation.
