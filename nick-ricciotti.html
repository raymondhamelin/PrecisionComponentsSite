Nick Ricciotti, a licensed mental health professional with a Master’s in Mental Health, serves as a therapist and advocate for first responders and veterans through Reps for Responders. Certified as a Behavioral Change Specialist, Nick blends therapy with personal development strategies to promote resilience and purpose. A husband and father, he values physical fitness, mindful meditation, and family time. His passion lies in helping individuals build mental and emotional strength.

Programs & Focus

The Comeback is Greater than the Setback (From rock bottom to thriving in all aspects of life, Nick discusses the importance of purpose in life while smashing the stigma surrounding the idea of asking for help for your mental health.)

Equipped for Life: Essential Elements for Resilience (The necessary steps individuals need to take to improve their quality of life so they can achieve success both personally and professionally. This PP can be tailored to the general public or the first responder/veteran community.

I started working with Nick after I reached out to Reps for Responders when I was struggling with my mental health and alcohol. I had just relocated from Arizona to NY and had felt like I lost my purpose. I was immediately able to build a connection with Nick, and he helped me feel like I was not alone in my troubles. Together, we worked on identifying character defects, implementing new healthy habits, and worked on my perspective and thought process. After a failed relationship in NY and with the help of Nick, I felt confident to make another major life decision and move back to Arizona. I was then able to find my purpose again as a helper, specifically a Firefighter. I am now employed as a fulltime Firefighter and feel like my old self again. Right now, I am exactly where I need to be in life and this would not be possible without Nick's help.
