Nick Ricciotti

Nick Ricciotti, a licensed mental health professional with a Master’s in Mental Health, serves as a therapist and advocate for first responders and veterans through Reps for Responders. Certified as a Behavioral Change Specialist, Nick blends therapy with personal development strategies to promote resilience and purpose. A husband and father, he values physical fitness, mindful meditation, and family time. His passion lies in helping individuals build mental and emotional strength.
