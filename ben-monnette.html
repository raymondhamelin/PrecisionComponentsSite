Ben Monnette

Ben Monnette, a former U.S. Army Green Beret, brings 16 years of Special Operations expertise and eight Middle Eastern deployments to his leadership work. A dedicated advocate for mental health, he shares lessons from overcoming personal struggles to inspire resilience and balanced living. With extensive coaching experience in high-stakes environments, Ben equips others with the tools to strengthen mental agility and achieve well-being in life’s most challenging situations.
